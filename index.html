Travel Intelligence Reports

Everything you need to know, before you go.

A good trip starts before you ever pack a bag. We put together a real intelligence briefing on your destination — the safety picture, current scams, health and entry details, and the things that actually matter once you land. Whether you're headed out for a vacation or a work trip, the goal is the same: you should feel like you know what to expect, not like you're guessing.

The reports

Two tiers, one standard of intelligence.

Every Waypoint report is built from the same research, at the same standard of care. The tiers differ in depth and freshness — never in access to the safety information that matters most.

Core coverage WPT · ESSENTIAL

Essential Intelligence Briefing

The core briefing: safety, scams, transportation, weather, and health, plus entry requirements, money, and communications guidance — with a city-level risk breakdown and any relevant personalization.

  • Security, Scams & Current Threats, Transportation, Weather, Health & Medical
  • Entry requirements, money & payments, communications
  • City-level risk breakdown
  • Personalized to what you tell us about your trip
Stays current until you leave WPT · SIGNATURE

Signature Intelligence Report

Everything in Essential, taken deeper — plus neighborhood-level detail, local laws and customs, and a pre-departure refresh that re-checks time-sensitive sections closer to your travel date.

  • Everything in Essential, at deep-dive depth with broader source cross-verification
  • Neighborhood-level risk breakdown
  • Cultural Considerations & Local Laws
  • Pre-departure refresh before you fly
A cheaper report never means a less-safe traveler. Both tiers include the full safety picture — Signature adds depth, granularity, and a refresh closer to departure, not information the Essential tier withholds.
Inside every report

What you actually get.

Same structure every time, so you always know where to look — only the depth changes by tier.

  • Situation Summary — one paragraph, plus a scannable risk-at-a-glance table
  • Neighborhood Risk Breakdown — a genuine differentiator, not a top-level summary
  • Safety & Security — the current picture on the ground
  • Scams & Current Threats — what's actually being reported now
  • Transportation, Weather, Health & Medical — practical, current guidance
  • Entry Requirements, Money & Payments, Communications — the logistics
  • Personalized section — for family travel, solo travel, mobility needs, and more
  • Emergency Planning card — a standalone quick-reference
  • Full source log — every finding labeled by confidence and sourced

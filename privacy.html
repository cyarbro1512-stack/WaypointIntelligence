This Privacy Policy explains how [Waypoint Travel Intelligence / Legal Entity Name] ("Waypoint," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

Anything you share with us — including any personal details — is used only to make your report more useful to you. We never sell or share your information for marketing purposes, and sensitive details are treated with the same care we'd want for our own trips.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact information: name and email address, provided when you submit an intake request or create an account.
  • Trip details: destination, travel dates, and the trip information you provide so we can research and prepare your report.
  • Personalization details: anything you choose to disclose that helps us tailor your report — for example, family or children traveling with you, solo travel, mobility needs, LGBTQ+ considerations, pets, business travel, or medical conditions relevant to your trip.
  • Payment information: handled directly by our payment processor, Stripe. We receive confirmation of payment but do not store your full card details.
  • Usage data: information about how you use the Service, such as pages visited.

2. How we use this information

  • To research and prepare your Essential Intelligence Briefing or Signature Intelligence Report, including the personalized section of your report.
  • To process payments.
  • To deliver your report and, for the Signature tier, its pre-departure refresh.
  • To communicate with you about your order or the Service.

We do not use the personal or sensitive details you disclose for marketing purposes, and we do not sell your information.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the Service, such as Stripe for payment processing, our intake form provider, and our hosting provider.
  • Legal authorities, where required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to prepare and deliver your report, provide any pre-departure refresh, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain uses of it. To make a request, contact us at waypointintel@gmail.com.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, especially the sensitive personal details you may share for personalization. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to waypointintel@gmail.com.
